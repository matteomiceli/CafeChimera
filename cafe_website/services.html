Welcome to our services at Cafe Chimera

Feel free to book with us at any time and we will get back to you as soon as possible. Any questions can be directed to our Contact Us Page

Kids Birthday Parties

We host kids parties from ages 8-12, usually in the late morning early afternoon. We have magicians, face painters, clowns, bouny castles and more. Let your little creatures run around in a safe environment with a chance to meet some of our adoptables that we board here at the cafe.

Wedding Venues

We have space for wedding receptions and after parties, dance floors and an amazing view at the top of the castle to say your vows. We have servers ready to help and a well stocked bar.

Rides

The rides here at Cafe Chimera are done in a supervised area with our staff to guide you on our array of rides. Choose from: Gary the Griffin glides, Sally the Unicorn trails and Splat the Kelpie surfs. They are all trained and super docile, very comfortable around humans and love to please. You can also treat them with their favourite snacks along the way.
